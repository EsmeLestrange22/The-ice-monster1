Get to know about the characters!

Elsie is a homeless orphan who lives on the streets of London

Dotty is the cleaning lady at the NATURAL HISTORY MUSEUM. She is as daft as her brushes.

Private Thomas is Dotty's boyfriend, the shortest soldier who ever served in the british army. His fellow soldiers call him TITCH. He is now retired and lives at the Royal Hospital Chelsea, making him a CHELSEA PENSIONER.

Commisioner Barker is the fearsome head of the London Metropolitan Police, famous for his tiny moustache.

The professor (Osbert Bertram Cuthbert Farnaby Beverly Smith) was- many years ago- the top scientist at the museum until one of his experiments went catastrophically wrong.

The colonel is also a CHELSEA PENSIONER.

Queen Victoria is the ruler of the British Empire. In 1899, she had ruled Britain for a staggering reign of sixty-two years.

Abdul Karim is always at the queen's side. He is her handsome young Indian attendant, also known as "Munshi".

Raj has his own confectionery emporium- or sweet trolly.

Wooly, the Ice Monster herself, she's a mammoth who died ten thousand years ago. The lifeless animal was discovered by arctic explorers, perfectly preserved in ice.
